## Instructions

- [ ] **Prep Pepperoni**: Slice the pepperoni into small, quartered pieces.
- [ ] **Build Cream Base**: In a large bowl, blend the softened cream cheese, sour cream, milk, and spices (garlic salt, pepper, red pepper flakes) until smooth.
- [ ] **Combine**: Add the quartered pepperoni, mozzarella, bell peppers, and onion to the cream mixture. Mix well to ensure even distribution.
- [ ] **Prep Dish**: Lightly grease a 2-quart casserole dish. Pour the mixture in and spread evenly.
- [ ] **Bake**: Place in a 350Â°F oven for 35â€“40 minutes until hot and bubbly.
- [ ] **Serve**: Serve warm with sliced French baguette, Ritz crackers, or pita chips.
