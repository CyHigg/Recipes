## Instructions

- [ ] **Prep Proteins**: Slice or shred your cooked chicken and slice your cooked sausage. Set both aside.
- [ ] **SautÃ© Base**: In a large pan over medium heat, add the sausage, garlic, and optional vegetables (peppers/onions). SautÃ© until warm and sizzling.
- [ ] **Build Sauce**: Add the heavy cream, blackening/cajun seasonings, and **optional spices (red pepper/paprika)**. Mix well.
- [ ] **Thicken**: Increase the temperature until the liquid reaches a boil. 
- [ ] **Integrate Chicken**: Add the cooked chicken to the boiling sauce and mix well to incorporate.
- [ ] **Combine**: Slowly add the cooked pasta to the pan. Toss until the noodles are fully coated.
- [ ] **Finish**: Add parmesan cheese to taste. Serve hot, ideally with fresh baked bread.
